Our Process

The Solutions

Planning

"A goal without a plan is just a wish" - We pay adequate attention to planning your website. We believe it is the key to al things that will happen in the future. We do not just take orders from you but add our suggestions and attempt to make the website better today. It helps us visualize the success.

01

Design

"It is not how good you are, it is about how good you want to be." - We use the state of the art design trends to define the best layout and design for your website. We believe that user experience is not just about an interface, but more about illustrating a journey. We bring a new you, a better You.

02

Managing Productivity

"80% of the work is done in 20% of the time". We believe that management of the resources defines the deadline. We bring all flavors to a team that defines the success factor and then delegate tasks between them. Time, priority management, and productivity tools lets us finish things in time.

03

Coding

"First solve the problem, then write the code." - We solve the problem, write the pseudo code, and then move to the final coding. Getting things done in the most optimum way is more important than getting things done. We use the latest tools for productivity and get our code reviewed by experienced developers.

04

Testing

"Good is not good enough, shoot for the best." - We have automated tools to test our code in dev, staging and production environments. On top of that, we manually go through all the functionalities and interfaces on different browsers and devices before confirming to you that things are done.

05

Documentation

"If you didn't document it, then you didn't do it." - It is our responsibility to write necessary comments in our code. We provide you with necessary documentation for all things we have built. A good document will let you maintain the website independent of vendors which is important.
